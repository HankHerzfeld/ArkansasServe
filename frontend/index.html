Community Service,
Simplified for Arkansas

Arkansas Serve connects students and juveniles with volunteer opportunities — and gives schools the tools to track and verify every hour.

📋 For Students

Browse events near you, sign up in one tap, and track your total service hours.

🏫 For Schools & JDCs

Approve hours, run reports, and stay compliant — all in one dashboard.

🤝 For Organizations

Post volunteer events, manage sign-ups, and log attendance with ease.

How It Works

1

Sign In

Use your school Microsoft 365 or Google account — no new password needed.

2

Find Events

Browse volunteer opportunities posted by trusted community organizations.

3

Serve & Track

Attend the event, get your hours logged, and watch your total grow.

4

School Verifies

Your school or JDC approves the hours — everything is documented and exportable.

For Schools & JDCs

Arkansas Serve gives administrators a real-time view of every student's service activity. Approve hours, flag issues, and pull compliance reports — all without spreadsheets or manual tracking.

  • Single sign-on with your existing Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace
  • Instant notification when hours are submitted for approval
  • Student roster with individual hour totals
  • Exportable reports for compliance and court documentation

For Community Organizations

Post volunteer events, manage student sign-ups, and submit hours on behalf of attendees — all through a simple portal designed to make coordination effortless.

  • Create and publish events in minutes
  • See who's registered and check students in on event day
  • Submit verified hours directly to the student's school
  • Upload photos and verification documents
